\section*{About}

The Recreational Mathematics Club is a relaxed seminar space for
exploring beautiful mathematics — with no prerequisites and no pressure.

We focus on:
\begin{itemize}
    \item elegant ideas
    \item surprising connections
    \item curiosity-driven discussions
\end{itemize}

Everyone is welcome.
